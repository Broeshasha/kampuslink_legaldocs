Community Guidelines

Last updated: January 2026

Kampuslink is a community platform. To keep it safe, users must follow these rules.

Prohibited Content

  • Harassment or hate speech
  • Violence or threats
  • Fraud, scams, or impersonation
  • Illegal goods or services
  • Pornographic or explicit material
  • Academic cheating services

Enforcement

Violations may result in content removal, suspension, or permanent bans. Enforcement decisions are final.
